TRAKI iOS · concept

A time tracker built for one thing: start studying in a single tap.

Playful Cards home — resume where you left off, or tap any mode to start the stopwatch instantly. Theme follows your system by default; switch to Light or Dark in Settings. Stats, history, timer and widgets all follow the theme. The prototype is live — tap a card to track, stop to see the recap, and use the tab bar for Stats, History and Settings.
